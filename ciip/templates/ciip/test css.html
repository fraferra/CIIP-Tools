About

CIIP is an internship program which offers top students from select international partner universities an opportunity to come to USA for a year to gain industry experience as part of their education. Interns work alongside Cisco employees on next-generation development projects and learn critical technical and business skills appropriate for a future in tomorrow's global workforce.

Apart from the workplace skills, interns return to their home country to complete their degrees with a much greater appreciation of global cultural issues and increased motivation to succeed in their academic and subsequent professional careers.
