Guidelines

  1. Once logged in please make sure your skills and interests are up-to-date in General Info in order for a list of best match applicants to be shown.

  2. Home will allow you to view a list of the applicants. You can narrow the selection by:

    • Search by specific skills (e.g. java, networks, etc.)
    • Filter by ranking (e.g. 1, 2, 3), status (e.g. interviewing, etc.), university (UCL, Tsinghua, etc.)

  3. For each applicant you can Download CV or Schedule Interview.

  4. Once Schedule Interview is selected your are shown the best time to contact that applicant and if they have other interviews.

  5. Once you have chosen a date and time, there is the option to interview via Skype, in which case please enter your skype name and select Schedule Interview at the bottom of the page, the student will recieve a notification email. Otherwise use Send Mail to set up a WebEx followed by Schedule Interview and the interview will be added to your Outlook.

  6. On the left will be a list of your scheduled interviews. Each hiring manager has a limit of 5 interviews scheduled at once. To remove and cancel an interview click x

  7. By selecting My Students on the left navigation bar you have the option to:

    • Download CV - review their CV
    • More Info and Interview Comment - enter comments from the interview
    • Send Message - send an instant message to the applicant

  8. Once you make an offer to a student the other managers will not be able to change the offer status.

  9. Please email ciip_office@cisco.com before you make an official offer.
